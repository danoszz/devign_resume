Personal work

Fly through streetlights in webVR

Check it out

Mobile WebVR Experience made with Three.js and D3.js

Making a public dataset alive by interfacing technology and design. What you see are the streetlights and their mapped data like height, coordinates and material. One big 3D scatterplot in webVR. It was the final exam of the course datavisualisation given by wooorm at the Amsterdam UoAS. Experimenting with Three.js and D3.js to be on the edge of both technologies.
